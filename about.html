About Us

Family-owned. Industry-focused. Partnership-driven.

Our Story

Built on relationships, not transactions

Flexi Personnel is a family business, and that changes how we operate. We don't take our connections to clients and candidates lightly. Every relationship is an investment, and we put our systems, processes, and people in place to ensure those relationships grow.

Our consultants don't sit behind desks filling orders. They visit worksites, they understand production floors, they know what a good hire looks like in your specific environment because they've been there.

With client partnerships stretching back over 20 years, we've earned the trust of businesses across manufacturing, construction, healthcare, and professional services, and we continue to earn it every day.
