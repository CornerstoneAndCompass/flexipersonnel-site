Production Line Supervisor

Food Manufacturing Co.
📍 Campbellfield, VIC 💰 $72,000 - $82,000 + super 📅 Posted 3 days ago

About the role

We are seeking an experienced Production Line Supervisor to join a well-established food manufacturing company in Campbellfield. Reporting to the Production Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing daily production operations across multiple lines, ensuring output targets are met safely and efficiently.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone with a hands-on approach who thrives in fast-paced manufacturing environments and enjoys leading teams to deliver consistent, high-quality results.

Key responsibilities

  • Supervise and coordinate daily production activities across assigned lines
  • Manage, mentor, and develop a team of 15-20 production workers
  • Ensure production targets, quality standards, and safety requirements are met
  • Conduct shift handovers and communicate key issues to management
  • Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency
  • Monitor and enforce compliance with OH&S policies and food safety standards
  • Coordinate with maintenance, quality, and logistics teams
  • Complete daily production reports and incident documentation

About you

  • 3+ years of experience in a production supervisor or team leader role
  • Background in food manufacturing, FMCG, or similar production environments
  • Strong leadership skills with the ability to motivate and develop teams
  • Solid understanding of OH&S legislation and food safety standards
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work rotating shifts as required
  • Forklift licence is desirable but not essential

What's on offer

  • Competitive salary of $72,000 - $82,000 plus superannuation
  • Permanent, full-time position with a stable, growing company
  • Career development opportunities within the organisation
  • Supportive team culture with strong leadership
  • Free on-site parking and close to public transport
